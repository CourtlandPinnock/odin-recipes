French Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 slices thick brioche
  • 2 eggs
  • 80ml whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Â½ tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 2 tbsp vegtable oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Toppings berries,icing sugar,etc (optional)

Instructions

  1. Beat together the eggs,milk,cream,vanilla and cinnamon
  2. In a shallow dish let the brioche slices soak in the egg mixture for 2-3 minutes, then carefully turn and further soak for 2 more minutes.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp of the oil and butter in a frying pan over a medium heat until foaming. Lift two slices of the brioche and add to the frying pan, take care when doing this.
  4. Fry until golden, roughly 3 minutes on each side. Place in a warm oven while you repeat for the rest.
  5. Serve hot and with your favorite toppings
